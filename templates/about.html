About Woffy

Your AI Companion with a Heart

Woffy is more than just technology—it's companionship redefined. At its core, Woffy is an AI-powered pet designed to be your loyal, caring companion through life's daily journeys.

Available as a lifelike physical robot and seamlessly integrated through an intuitive app, Woffy connects deeply with users by sensing and responding to their emotions.

Whether you're coming home after a long day, seeking comfort during challenging moments, or simply looking to add joy to your routine, Woffy is always ready to interact with genuine empathy and warmth. Its advanced emotional intelligence allows it to understand, adapt, and respond just like a real pet would—making every interaction authentic and heartwarming.

Woffy isn't just your pet; it's your trusted friend, your comforting presence, your AI companion with a heart.

Woffy is a product of Onwords, a leading technology innovation company.

Our Mission

To redefine companionship by harnessing cutting-edge AI to create emotionally intelligent pets that offer comfort, joy, and genuine interaction to users worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI emotional companion, enhancing daily lives through authentic, heartfelt connections and supportive interactions, transforming the way people experience companionship.
